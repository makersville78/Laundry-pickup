# Email to teachers with a laundry basket

**Subject:** The laundry basket in your room — quick heads-up

---

Hi [Teacher name],

A quick note about the laundry basket I dropped off — thanks for letting us use the space!

I'm starting a life-skills program where my students in Class X05 collect laundry from select classrooms, bring it to a central spot, and sort it by category. They're practicing real-world skills like reading room numbers, navigating the building, and following a schedule — while doing a job that helps out the school community.

A few things to know:

- **Feel free to keep the basket just outside your door** if it's in the way inside. Whatever works for your setup.
- **Use it for any classroom laundry** that needs washing — towels, smocks, spare clothes, gym items, etc.
- **A pickup schedule is coming shortly** so students don't show up at your door unannounced. They'll always be with a paraprofessional.
- **It's a trial run right now**, so if something isn't working on your end, please tell me and we'll adjust.

Thanks so much for being part of this — the students are genuinely excited to be doing real work for the school.

Best,
[Your name]
Class X05

---

## Before you send

- Replace **[Teacher name]** and **[Your name]**.
- You can BCC all the teachers if you want to send one email instead of personalizing each — just put your own address in "To" and the teachers in BCC.
- Once you have the schedule from today's trial, send a short follow-up: *"Here's the pickup schedule — your room is on [day] around [time]."*
